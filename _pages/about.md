Yisong Xiao is a fourth-year PhD student at the State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment, Beihang University (BUAA), supervised by Prof. <a href='https://xlliu-beihang.github.io/'>Xianglong Liu</a> and Prof. <a href='https://liuaishan.github.io/index.html'>Aishan Liu</a>.

My research interests include LLM safety, fairness testing and mitigation, as well as toxicity mitigation.
